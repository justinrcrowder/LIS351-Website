About the Platies

I wanted to include some bigger fish in my tank, but I was limited in options based on my tank's size. This made the platies a good choice because they aren't small like the guppies, and they won't grow too large for my tank. Sometimes, the platies get a little agressive while eating. Otherwise, I only see them picking at the mystery snails in my tank. Hopefully they aren't inflicting too much harm on the snails.
